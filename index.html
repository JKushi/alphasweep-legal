AlphaSweep Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

AlphaSweep ("the app") is a word puzzle game developed for players of all ages. This page explains what information the app interacts with and how.

No account required

AlphaSweep does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to play.

Information stored on your device

Your game progress — score, coins, streaks, settings, and a short local error log used for troubleshooting — is stored only on your device, using standard on-device app storage. This information is never transmitted to us or to any server we operate. If you uninstall the app or clear its storage, this data is permanently deleted.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminder notifications, they are scheduled locally on your device. No data about your play habits is sent anywhere to generate or deliver these reminders.

Advertising

AlphaSweep displays ads through Google AdMob (banner, interstitial, and optional rewarded ads that let you earn in-game hints or shuffles). Because AlphaSweep is available to a general audience that may include children, every ad request is tagged for child-directed treatment, so all players — regardless of age — are shown only non-personalized, contextual ads. No behavioral or interest-based ad targeting is used. AdMob and its partners may still collect limited technical information (such as a device identifier and general device/usage information) needed to deliver and measure these non-personalized ads, in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's data collection practices. We do not control or have access to this data ourselves.

Children's privacy

AlphaSweep is available to a general audience that may include children under 13. The app does not require an account and does not collect personal information from any player, including children. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play Families Policy, every player is shown only non-personalized, contextual advertising as described above — no ad personalization or behavioral tracking is used for anyone.

Data sharing and sale

We do not sell your personal information. We do not operate any backend server that receives your gameplay data. The only data leaving your device is what Google AdMob independently collects for ad delivery, described above.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
